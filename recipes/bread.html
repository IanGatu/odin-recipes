Crust Issues

Description

This bread recipe yields a crusty exterior with a soft and airy interior. Perfect for sandwiches or as a side to soups and stews.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast (2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 450째F (230째C). Place a baking stone or an inverted baking sheet in the oven to heat up.
  6. Punch down the risen dough and shape it into a round loaf. Place it on a piece of parchment paper.
  7. Make a few slashes on the top of the loaf with a sharp knife to allow steam to escape during baking.
  8. Carefully transfer the parchment paper with the loaf onto the preheated baking stone or sheet in the oven.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  10. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.
